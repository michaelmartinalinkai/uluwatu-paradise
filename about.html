About Uluwatu Paradise

European expertise meets authentic Balinese craftsmanship — creating extraordinary living spaces in Bali's most coveted location.

Our Story

Uluwatu Paradise is the flagship project of Next Gen Home (NGH Property Group), a property development company with over 10 years of experience in real estate, investment, and construction. We bring European standards of quality and design thinking to Bali's stunning south coast.

Our team combines deep knowledge of international property markets with strong local partnerships in Bali. From cafes and restaurants to architects and investors, we have built a network that ensures every project is delivered to the highest standards.

Our Philosophy: "If you can dream it, we can build it." We believe luxury living should be attainable, sustainable, and rooted in the beauty of its surroundings.

Why Uluwatu?

Known as the "Beverly Hills of Bali," Uluwatu offers dramatic cliff-top views, world-class surf breaks, and an increasingly sophisticated lifestyle scene. Property values in Uluwatu have shown consistent growth, making it one of Bali's most attractive investment locations.

Our development sits in Pantai Balangan, Ungasan — a prime location with proximity to beaches, temples, and Bali's best restaurants, while remaining peaceful and private.

Our Values

Quality Craftsmanship

We combine authentic Balinese architecture with Western functionality. Local natural materials and traditional building techniques meet modern engineering standards.

Investment Value

Our properties are designed for both living and investing. With projected ROI of 10-14% at 70% occupancy, Uluwatu Paradise delivers both lifestyle and returns.

Community First

We work closely with local communities, partnering with Balinese artisans, suppliers, and businesses. Every project strengthens the local economy.

Full-Service Experience
