Best Sunset Spots in Uluwatu: Where to Watch the Magic

April 10, 2026 · Uluwatu Paradise

Why Uluwatu is a Sunset Lover's Paradise

Uluwatu, Bali, offers some of the most breathtaking sunset views, making it a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the magic of a tropical evening. Nestled on the island's southern tip, Uluwatu sunset spots are renowned for their stunning ocean views and dramatic cliffside settings.

Top Uluwatu Sunset Spots You Can't Miss

1. Uluwatu Temple

Perched on a steep cliff, Uluwatu Temple is not only a spiritual landmark but also one of the best places to catch a sunset. The temple's location offers panoramic views of the Indian Ocean, making it a prime spot for both photographers and travelers. Arrive early to secure a good vantage point and enjoy the traditional Kecak dance performed at sunset.

2. Single Fin

This iconic bar is situated on the cliffs of Uluwatu, offering a relaxed vibe and stunning ocean vistas. Known for its lively atmosphere and live music, Single Fin is a favorite among tourists and locals alike. Sipping on a cocktail while watching the sun dip below the horizon is an experience not to be missed.

3. Karang Boma Cliff

For those seeking a more secluded experience, Karang Boma Cliff is the perfect spot. With fewer crowds, it provides a peaceful setting to enjoy the natural beauty of an Uluwatu sunset. Be sure to bring a picnic and enjoy the tranquility as the sky transforms into a canvas of colors.

4. Jimbaran Bay

Although slightly outside Uluwatu, Jimbaran Bay is worth the short drive. The beach here is lined with seafood restaurants where you can enjoy a romantic dinner on the sand as the sun sets.

FAQs About Uluwatu Sunset Spots

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Uluwatu Temple for sunset?

Arrive at least an hour before sunset to explore and secure a good spot.

Are there any entry fees for Uluwatu Temple?

Yes, there is a small entry fee, which also includes the Kecak dance performance.

Can I book a villa with sunset views?

Yes, many luxury villas in Uluwatu offer spectacular sunset views from private pools.
